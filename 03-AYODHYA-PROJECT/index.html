Exhibition

Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh hosts many exhibitions, including: Deepotsav:- Also known as Diwali, this festival is celebrated a day before Diwali, and includes a cultural heritage walk, the lighting of thousands of earthen lamps, a staged Ramleela, and the Grand Aarti. Shravan Jhula Fair:- This fair takes place from the third day of the second half of Shravan, which is between July and August, until the end of the month. During this fair, images of deities, particularly Lord Ram, Sita, and Lakshman, are placed on swings in temples throughout Ayodhya. Devotees also sing praises of Lord Rama throughout the procession. Pran Pratishtha:- This ceremony was held on January 22, 2024, and is expected to be inaugurated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi. Ayodhya also hosts other cultural programs, such as an art camp based on Lord Ram and the Ramcharitmanas, and an exhibition on lifestyle for the environment.
